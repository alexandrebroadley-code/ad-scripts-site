01

Registration decline

New monthly registrations peaked at 6,118 in March 2025 and have fallen to an estimated ~470 in April 2026 — a 92% decline over 13 months. The drop has been steady and consistent across every tracker.

Ad spend & cost efficiency

Ad spend fell from a high of $48,563 in June 2025 to $10,845 in February 2026. Registrations followed spend down, however the cost per registration also increased — rising from $10 in May 2025 to $28 in October 2025 — indicating the spend reduction is not the only factor at play.

03

Revenue & profitability

Despite falling registrations, revenue from Hollywoodbets has remained robust — peaking at $46,155 in December 2025. The existing player base is highly active and valuable. Gross profit turned consistently positive from September 2025 onwards, reaching $34,804 in December.

04

Player base — April 2026

The April 2026 player report shows 8,592 unique players generating R9.13M in deposits and R438,603 in commission in the first 19 days alone. The player base is predominantly returning players — new sign-ups account for a small fraction of both activity and revenue.

05

Returning player growth

Despite declining new registrations, the returning player base has grown from 7,100 in August 2025 to 8,296 in April 2026 — a total growth of +16.8%. The standout jump was December 2025 to March 2026 (+18.0%), showing the retained player pool is expanding even as new acquisition slows.
