Key insights & recommendations
CAC efficiency — watch closely
Cost per FTD dropped 45% from Feb ($20) to Mar ($11) as volume scaled. Target sub-$10 CAC to bring breakeven inside 6 months. Focus budget on the channels delivering Mar-level efficiency.
CPA cohort — highest lever
250 Hybrid-deal players (250) earn both a flat CPA fee ($2/player) and ongoing RevShare — $14.79 avg net revenue each vs. $0.01 for non-qualifying players. Growing the number of players who hit the CPA threshold is the single highest-impact action to improve LTV.
Volume trajectory — strong
FTDs +245%, deposits +374%, revenue +334% in one month. The ramp is steep and positive. April and May data will confirm whether this is sustained or a one-off spike.
Concentration risk
Top 5 players = 73% of net revenue. One player alone = 53%. LTV is highly skewed. Work with the operator to diversify value across the depositing base rather than relying on a small cluster.
Negative-value players
93 players (12.5%) show negative net revenue — they won more than they wagered. Review promo and bonus exposure on these accounts. Worst case is −$801 from a single player.
Bet9ja comparison (same market)
Bet9ja NG is profitable with $15,961 GP on $13,844 ad spend (1.15× ROI) after an extended ramp. BetKing has higher per-player upside but needs 2–3 more months to confirm its trajectory.
