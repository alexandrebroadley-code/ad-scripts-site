Registration to FTD funnel

Of every 100 registrations, 70 make a first deposit and 30 never convert. Of those 70, only around 57 go on to generate any commission. The $15 cost per registration understates the real acquisition cost — it should be viewed as $26 per first time depositor.

LTV per first time depositor

Based on 6,572 FTDs active in 3 or more months. LTV is monthly commission multiplied by projected lifespan. The median FTD generates just R19/month (~$1) — well below what it costs to acquire them at $26.

ROI analysis

Measured against $26 per FTD, the median depositing player does not pay back their acquisition cost over 18 months. Profitability is entirely driven by the top 30–40% of FTDs. Acquiring more players matters less than acquiring better quality players.

The median FTD loses money at 0.7x ROI

A typical depositing player generates $19 in commission over 18 months against a $26 acquisition cost. The business is only profitable in aggregate because a small number of high-value players more than compensate for the majority.

FTD value distribution

71.8% of FTDs generate under R50/month — below break-even. Only the top 10% (657 players) generate enough to carry the business. The top 10% produce 75% of all commission revenue.

Monthly cost per FTD

Cost per FTD varies significantly month to month — from $17.46 in December 2025 to $40.79 in October 2025. October was the worst month for acquisition efficiency. The overall weighted average across the full period is $26.03 per FTD.

Methodology

How these numbers were calculated
StepMethod
Data source7 monthly player-level affiliate reports (Aug 2025 – Apr 2026)
Player filterFTDs only, active in 3+ months with positive commission — 6,572 players
Monthly commissionTotal commission ÷ number of active months per player
LTV projectionMonthly commission × estimated lifespan (12–24 months)
Lifespan estimateCohort analysis: players from Jun 2024 still active Apr 2026 (22 months)
FTD conversion rate16,336 FTDs ÷ 23,348 total registrations = 70%
Cost per FTD$284,898 total spend ÷ 10,947 FTDs (Performix data) = $26.03
Cost per registration$284,898 ÷ 18,925 registrations = $15.05
CurrencyZAR converted at R18.50 = $1.00

Note: data covers 7 non-consecutive months. Jan and Feb 2026 player-level reports were unavailable. The period (Aug 2025 – Apr 2026) was a declining traffic period — peak LTV may be higher.
